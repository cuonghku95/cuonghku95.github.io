[cite_start]I am a PhD Candidate in the **Department of Urban Planning and Design** at **The University of Hong Kong (HKU)**, supported by the prestigious **Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Award (HKPFS)** and the **HKU Presidential PhD Scholarship**[cite: 3, 149, 150]. [cite_start]My research focuses on leveraging spatial data science and artificial intelligence to foster smarter, healthier, and more vibrant urban environments[cite: 4, 10].

[cite_start]Currently, I am also a Visiting PhD Scholar at the **School of Geographical and Earth Sciences, University of Glasgow**, UK (Oct - Nov 2025)[cite: 11, 13, 14]. [cite_start]Prior to my doctoral studies, I served as a Lecturer and Academic Advisor at **Vietnam National University, Hanoi (VNU)**, where I also obtained my Master's and Bachelor's degrees in Land Administration, both graduating 1st in the class with High Distinction[cite: 15, 17, 20, 21, 23, 25, 26].

Research Interests
------
[cite_start]My research interests lie at the intersection of urban analytics, geographic information science, and urban planning[cite: 4, 10]:
* **Urban Health & Vitality:** Analyzing how built environments affect human activities and well-being[cite: 4, 5].
* [cite_start]**GeoAI (Geospatial Artificial Intelligence):** Applying machine learning/deep learning (e.g., object detection, semantic segmentation) to urban studies[cite: 5, 51, 53].
* [cite_start]**Transit-Oriented Development (TOD):** Examining the socio-economic and spatial impacts of urban rail and transport infrastructures[cite: 5, 62, 63].
