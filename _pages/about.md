Research Interests
------
* **Urban Health & Vitality:** Analyzing how built environments affect human activities and well-being.
* **GeoAI (Geospatial Artificial Intelligence):** Applying machine learning/deep learning (e.g., object detection, semantic segmentation) to urban studies.
* **Transit-Oriented Development (TOD):** Examining the socio-economic and spatial impacts of urban rail and transport infrastructures.
