I am deeply honored and grateful to receive the **HKU Foundation First Year Excellent PhD Award 2024/25** from The University of Hong Kong. This prestigious award is presented annually to approximately 15 exceptional PhD students across the entire university, recognizing outstanding academic performance and research potential during their initial year of doctoral studies.

Receiving this recognition is a significant milestone in my academic journey at HKU. It serves as an incredible encouragement for my ongoing research at the intersection of urban analytics, geographic information science, and sustainable urban planning. 

I would like to express my heartfelt gratitude to my supervisors, mentors, and colleagues at the Department of Urban Planning and Design for their invaluable guidance, constant support, and inspiration. I look forward to contributing more meaningful findings to the field of urban dynamics and sustainability in the coming years.
